What is Intentionally Budgeting

The Intentionally Budgeting Calculator is a tool designed to create intention for budgeting, by prompting users to find information they may not know of the top of their head. This makes users think about how much they need to spend, creating more financial self-awareness. Combined with resources about making a budget using that information, Intentionally Budgeting aims to help make become more financially literate.

Why is this Helpful

According to survey by Intuit Mint 65 percent of Americans said they don't know how much they spend last month. Additionally, that survey found that 31% of those who do know how much they spend regret how much they spent. They survey also found that 62% of people who have a budget felt more confident about money. By creating more financial self-awareness, Intentionally budgeting hopes to help people feel more confident and make better spending decisions.
