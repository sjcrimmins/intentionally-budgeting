How to Use the Intentionally Budgeting Calculator

  1. Determine your monthly income after taxes
  2. Determine your monthly cost of living (this is the cost of all of your necessities each month)
  3. Determine your monthly savings/investment (how much money you want to save or invest each month)
  4. Use the results for the calculator to make a budget
